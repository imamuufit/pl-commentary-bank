## Commentary-safe venue notes

The official program confirms the event was held on 2026-06-21 at 北海道沼田町民会館, hosted by 一般社団法人北海道パワーリフティング協会 and managed by キムンカムイ旭川.

The program includes official local framing useful for commentary, including "まつりばやしとほたるの里 沼田町", official support from 北海道, 沼田町, 沼田町教育委員会, 沼田町スポーツ協会, and 沼田町商工会, and a Numata-side greeting stating the town population as 2,700.

The program also states that a Hokkaido-level meet in Numata was being held for the first time since 2022, and that Numata hosted the Hokkaido-Tohoku block meet in 2023 and 2024.
